Silent Echo

Type

Commercial

Client

Echo Audio

Year

2021

Silent Echo is a high-end commercial for Echo Audio's flagship noise-canceling headphones. The visual approach focuses on creating a sense of absolute tranquility through minimal, carefully composed frames.

Using a combination of macro photography and fluid camera movements, we crafted a series of mesmerizing sequences that emphasize the product's premium quality and its ability to create personal moments of peace.
