Neon Nights

Type

Music Video

Artist

Luna Eclipse

Year

2022

Neon Nights is a vibrant exploration of nightlife and youth culture. Shot over three nights in downtown Los Angeles, we created a hypnotic visual journey that mirrors the pulsing energy of the music.

The cinematography embraces bold color choices and dynamic camera movements, creating a seamless blend of choreographed sequences and authentic moments captured in the city after dark.
